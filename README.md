### The main features of our app are:
```
- FlutterChittagong Bhromon App is a versatile mobile application developed using the Flutter framework, specifically designed to assist tourists and travellers exploring the picturesque city of Chittagong in Bangladesh. The app incorporates several powerful features to enhance the user experience and provide essential functionalities for planning and enjoying a memorable trip to Chittagong.

- The app seamlessly integrates Firebase as the backend service, enabling robust functionalities such as email verification authentication and profile updates. This ensures a secure and reliable user registration and login process, allowing users to create accounts, log in, and effortlessly manage their profiles.

- One of the key highlights of the app is the inclusion of a tour spot favorite section. Users can access a comprehensive list of popular tourist spots in Chittagong, complete with detailed overviews, captivating images, and ratings. By marking their favorite spots, users can conveniently organize and plan their itineraries. The app also provides directions and maps, aiding users in navigating to their desired destinations with ease.

- The app features dedicated screens for tour spot details, providing users with an immersive experience. The overview screen offers a concise summary of each tour spot, showcasing its key attractions and highlights. Users can delve deeper into the details by accessing the description screen, which provides comprehensive information about the tour spot, including historical significance, notable features, and tips for visitors.

- To further enhance the user experience, the app incorporates a food item section. This feature allows users to explore and discover local culinary delights and popular food items in Chittagong. Users can access a curated list of restaurants and eateries, along with mouth-watering descriptions and images of various food items. This section helps users in experiencing the vibrant culinary scene of the city.

- The app also includes an admin panel, granting authorized administrators the ability to manage the application's content. Administrators can effortlessly add new tour spots to the app, ensuring that the information remains up to date and relevant for users. This feature empowers the app to adapt to new attractions and maintain its freshness over time.

- In summary, FlutterChittagong Bhromon App is a comprehensive and user-friendly platform that caters to the needs of tourists and travelers visiting Chittagong. With its features like Firebase-powered authentication, profile management, tour spot favorites, tour spot details with overview and description screens, food item section, and an admin panel for content management, the app offers an all-in-one solution for users looking to explore the enchanting city of Chittagong and make their trip memorable.

```
